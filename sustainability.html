Sustainable Development Goals

Our actions are motivated by Gais Capitaism; a principle that combines the financial and humanitarian aspects of our shared world. Our belief is that every organisation needs to play a role in making a positive change in the world. Manutratech Industries is committed to being a part of that change. Manutratech in partnership with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals We believe that the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals can be achieved if we are intentional about them and if we all played a part to ensure they are achieved in a sustainable manner. We are working to craft a framework that can be able to fully address the United Nations Sustainable Goals and adopted world over by the year 2029. We are however already focused on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals in the following ways primarily:

Clean Water & Sanitation

We are currently involved in ensuring that sanitation is observed by making our products more and more accessible to people from all walks of life. We are working to form partnerships with organisations that turn dirty water into clean water that is suitable for water consumption. We are also working to start water harvesting as so much water goes into the drain during the rainy season in Africa and very few people have been able to successfully.

Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

As an Industrial & Innovative company, it is our mission to further industrialize our continent and impact the future generations to continue with industry and innovation to be competitive in a fast paced and dynamic world. It is within our vision to have an Industrial & Innovation Academy that is able to realize the hidden potential of people that may never be realized if not harnessed in the right way.

Climate Action

Climate change is becoming a reality to almost everyone who once thought that it was a scam. The earth is already showing signs of resistance by the earthquakes, typhoons, hurricanes and floods being witnessed across the world. If we do not act now, the situation is only going to get worse with greenhouse gas emissions at an all time high and vegetation cover is lessening. Our simplistic approach to Climate Action involves trying to reset our world to the way it was before it became one big concrete jungle.

About Manutratech Industries

Manutratech Industries, a company established in 2019 with a need to create value based products and services made in Africa and that compete on a global scale. Our strategy is to put Africa on the global map as a key supplier of manufactured household products through our Clean Haven Brand.
