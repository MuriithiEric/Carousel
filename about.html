Our Values and Manifesto

Who we Are


Lions
Why the Lion? The Lion is fearless and despite what comes in the way of the lion, the lion will always take his chances. No matter how big the opponent is. Just like the lion, we are not afraid to take on giants bigger than we are. While the lion may retreat after realizing that the opponent is bigger and stronger, the lio will realise that it is the strenght in numbers that enables them to be successful. Just like lions, we work as a close unit and team. Nobody is ever left behind.

Our Values

We are lions and that's what separates us from the rest in the jungle. As the king of the jungle we have a role to play in setting the pace for the others. Our principles are our guiding tower:

1.People

Our company is its people. We value each and every person for their individual and combined contribution to our success. We are unconventional in our approach for the pursuit of the best people and we believe in what you are capable of becoming rather than what you have been.

2.Excellence

We believe in being the best version of ourselves everyday and reflecting that in the work that we do.

3.Creativity

The world around us was born out of imagination. When we let our imagination run wild and be limitless, we are capable of creating anything.

4.Customer Centrism

We put the needs of our customers first. Our customers include suppliers, staff, community and stakeholders. Our key to a happy, healthy and productive society lies in our giving of a great experience.

5.Integrity

There are no shortcuts to any place worth going. We take out time to ensure we do the right thing and live within our means. We believe that we can be minimalist and still have an abundance mentality.

6. Sustainable Development

In everything that we do we are always crafting solutions to bring about sustainable change to our planet.

Our Manifesto

We are givers.

We are doers.

We believe in being the change that we want to see in our world.

We belive in holding each other and ourselves accountable.

About Manutratech Industries

Manutratech Industries, a company established in 2019 with a need to create value based products and services made in Africa and that compete on a global scale. Our strategy is to put Africa on the global map as a key supplier of manufactured household products through our Clean Haven Brand.
